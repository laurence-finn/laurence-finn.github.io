About Me

  • I am a technical writer with 18 years of experience working for NASA and DoD contractors. I live in Houston, Texas with my wife Jennifer and my ten-year-old cat Joey, who enjoys sleeping and "editing" leather furniture. When I'm not doing technical writing, I enjoy playing piano, programming, creative writing, and playing board games with friends (especially Magic: the Gathering).

  • Most semesters, I work as an adjunct English professor. I teach Composition I and II, as well as Technical Writing. My guiding principle is anyone can (and everyone should) master the craft of writing--all it takes is patience and practice, and the rewards are great. The most successful people I've met are excellent communicators, no matter their field.
